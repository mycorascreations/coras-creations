Meet the Creator

Hi, I'm Cora! I'm a young artist with a passion for bringing magical things to life. I started making resin art because I loved how you could trap color, glitter, and light inside something beautiful — like catching a little piece of a galaxy in your hands.

My 3D printing obsession started when I discovered I could make articulated dragons that actually move. Now I spend my free time designing, printing, and painting one-of-a-kind pieces for people who love fantasy and handmade things as much as I do.

Every single item in my shop is made by me, in my craft studio. No mass production — just a girl, her tools, and a whole lot of imagination. ✨
